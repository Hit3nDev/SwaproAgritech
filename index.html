1 Why Seaweed

What is the fuss about seaweed,
and why now?

Seaweeds are simply plants grown in salty sea water. Although seaweed has been around for ages, its many hidden benefits and applications have only come to light recently. It can easily be an answer to many of the socio-ecological problems being faced by humankind.

Read moreRead more

Seaweed absorbs CO₂ up to 20× faster per unit area than land-based plants, needs no freshwater or fertiliser, and releases around 70% more oxygen. It doesn't compete with food crops for land, and naturally sinks carbon to the seafloor when it dies.

Food & beverages, animal feed, biostimulants for agriculture, biofuels, bioplastics, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and textile fibres. More than 500 commercial products are derived from seaweed globally today.

India has 7,500 km of coastline, ideal water temperatures, and an established coastal fishing community. The National Seaweed Mission is already backing expansion, with Tamil Nadu — where Swapro operates — as the leading state.

6Our Product

Jaltaru — 100% natural seaweed biostimulant.

Jaltaru 100% Natural Seaweed-Based Biostimulant

Jaltaru is a 100% natural seaweed extract biostimulant developed with the patented technology of the prestigious CSIR-CSMCRI Institute.

This seaweed extract is a treasure trove of naturally occurring nutrients required for plant growth. Biostimulants, unlike fertilizers, do not directly provide synthetic nutrients to the plants but instead help the plants better absorb nutrients from the soil itself. When applied alongside fertilizers, it yields truly promising results — while reducing the usage of chemical fertilizers and maintaining soil health.

Jaltaru contains macro and micro nutrients, growth hormones, soluble proteins, enzymes, and minerals like potassium. It has a high concentration of potash, a vital organic nutrient involved in many metabolic processes in plants. Its prescribed application has resulted in better fruit size, leaf thickness, and stem strength.

Mandapam · with CSIR-CSMCRI

Proprietary Circular HDPE Rafts

Developed in collaboration with CSIR-CSMCRI Mandapam and successfully tested over multiple cultivation cycles, with measurable gains over traditional bamboo rafts.

  • Reduced cultivation cycle — 25–30 days vs. 45 days
  • 4–5× higher yield compared to bamboo rafts
  • Lifespan of ~12 years with minimal maintenance
  • Suitable for round-the-year cultivation and monsoon preservation
  • Potential for co-culture of allied species
