1Infrastructure

What does Swapro do?

We are the one stop solution providers for Seaweed Cultivation Infrastructure. We help in developing and deploying modern seaweed cultivation infrastructure suitable for different marine conditions for maximum yield.

We help in providing complete turn key solutions from site selection, designing, development, deployment, training, post harvesting marketing and value addition.

We have in-house design and manufacturing facility for developing Durable HDPE based Rafts, Integrated Multi-Trophic Aquaculture (IMTA) and other cultivation and post harvest infrastructure.

2About Us

Thirty years of roots,
one new tide to ride.

Swapro is a group of people passionate about the seaweed industry and engaged primarily in providing commercial seaweed farming solutions.

Though we are a startup, our roots come from Das & Kumar, our sister concern and one of India's most reputed names in aquaculture with over 30 years of experience in implementing projects for central and state governments, premier research institutes and private companies. Over three decades, the company has executed hundreds of projects across 20 Indian states and 5 countries.

We wish to replicate the stellar record in the seaweed industry.
