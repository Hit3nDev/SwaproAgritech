2Why HDPE

Why HDPE-based modern rafts?

①

Highly durable and sturdy structure, making it suitable for rough sea and weather conditions.

②

Can be placed farther from shore — allowing more area for cultivation and better yield.

③

Suitable for monsoon preservation of seedlings — one of the biggest challenges in cultivation.

④

Offers 4 times the yield per square metre compared to traditional bamboo rafts.

⑤

Long life of more than 10 years, with minimum maintenance required.
